What are Hyaluronic Acid Fillers?

Hyaluronic acid fillers restore volume, smooth wrinkles, and define contours with immediate and reversible results. They can be applied to lips, cheekbones, dark circles, chin, and nasolabial folds, adapted to each patient for a natural effect.

What are they used for?

  • Lip filler: volume, hydration, and contour.
  • Nasolabial folds: diminishes lines from nose to mouth.
  • Sunken dark circles: improves tired appearance.
  • Cheekbones and chin: harmonizes facial contour.
