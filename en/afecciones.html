What We Treat

Dark Circles

Dark circles are treated with fillers and laser to improve pigmentation and circulation.

Wrinkles

Wrinkles result from collagen loss with aging. They are treated with botulinum toxin, fillers, and CO2 laser to smooth skin and restore firmness.

Cellulite

Cellulite forms due to fat accumulation in localized areas. Treatments like mesotherapy, radiofrequency, or enzymes help reduce cellulite appearance.

Facial Sagging

Improves with radiofrequency and fillers for firmness.

Body Sagging

Treated with radiofrequency and Sculptra to tone.

Nasolabial Fold

Treated with fillers to smooth deep lines.

Jowls

Facial sagging is improved with radiofrequency and fillers.

Scars

Scars are treated with peeling, Erbium laser, or platelet-rich plasma.

Thin Lips

Lip filler improves volume and shape with hyaluronic acid.

Skin Spots

Skin spots are treated with laser, peeling, or pulsed light to improve tone and texture.

Double Chin

Double chin is treated with enzymes, radiofrequency, or laser, reducing fat and improving firmness.

Tattoos

Q-Switch laser removes tattoos by shattering ink particles in safe sessions.

Localized Fat

Localized fat is reduced with radiofrequency, mesotherapy, or enzymes.

Crow's Feet

Treated with botox to smooth deep lines.

Forehead Wrinkles

Forehead wrinkles are treated with botulinum toxin to relax muscles and smooth expression lines.

Marionette Lines

Marionette lines, which form from the corners of the lips to the chin, are treated with dermal fillers to smooth.

Aging

Aging is treated with botulinum toxin, fillers, and laser to improve firmness and reduce wrinkles.

Acne

Acne and its scars can be treated with chemical peel, intense pulsed light, or Q-Switch laser to improve texture and appearance of affected skin.

Barcode Wrinkles

Barcode wrinkles are treated with hyaluronic acid fillers and laser to smooth perioral lines.

Bruxism

Bruxism is treated with botulinum toxin to reduce muscle tension and relieve jaw pressure.

Gummy Smile

Gummy smile is corrected with botulinum toxin to relax the muscle that lifts the upper lip, reducing gum exposure.

Rosacea

Rosacea is treated with intense pulsed light or specific lasers to reduce redness and inflammation, improving overall skin appearance.

Unwanted Facial Hair

Unwanted hair removal is performed using lasers like Soprano Ice, guaranteeing effective hair reduction in various body areas.

Photoaging

Photoaging, caused by sun exposure, is treated with laser, pulsed light, or peels.
