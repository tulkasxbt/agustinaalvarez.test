What is body contouring?

Body contouring is a set of non-invasive treatments designed to improve the body's appearance. It helps reduce localized adiposity, decrease abdominal fat and improve skin texture, combating cellulite.

In our center we use Accent Prime, an advanced technology that combines radiofrequency and ultrasound to offer visible results in less time, without the need for surgery or prolonged recovery times.

How is the procedure?

During the session, a device combining radiofrequency and ultrasound is used to generate a thermal effect in the deep layers of the skin. This process helps break down fat deposits, improve circulation, and stimulate collagen production.

The session is comfortable and painless, and its duration varies according to the area to treat, but generally ranges between 30 and 60 minutes.

How many sessions do you need?

The number of sessions varies according to each patient's goal. On average, between 4 and 6 sessions are recommended, with an interval of 2 weeks between each one.

Results are progressive and are maximized from the third session onwards, reaching their full effect around three months later.

Body contouring results

After the treatment, you will notice a reduction of fat in the treated areas, firmer and more toned skin, and an improvement in the general texture of the skin.

To maintain long-term results, it is recommended to maintain a balanced diet and perform physical activity regularly.
