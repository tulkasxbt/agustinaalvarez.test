What is cellulite?

Cellulite is a condition that affects the texture of the skin, forming small depressions and the appearance of "orange peel". It is common in areas such as thighs, buttocks, and abdomen. Although it does not represent a health problem, many people seek to reduce its appearance to improve the tone and smoothness of their skin.

In our center we use Accent Prime, radiofrequency, and ultrasound that act on deep layers to improve the appearance of cellulite and tone the skin.
