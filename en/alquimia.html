Alquimia: renewal and luminosity for your skin

Alquimia is a skin renewal treatment that combines microdermabrasion and chemical exfoliation to remove dead cells, improve skin texture, and stimulate regeneration in a safe and controlled manner.

‚ú® What benefits does Alquimia have?

  • Improves texture and provides softness.
  • Gives luminosity to dull skin.
  • Attenuates fine wrinkles and mild expression lines.
  • Reduces superficial spots and dilated pores.
  • Helps improve mild acne scars.
  • Stimulates cell renewal and collagen.

‚öô How is the procedure?

The procedure lasts 30 to 45 minutes. Initial cleansing is performed, followed by microdermabrasion with the Alquimia equipment and, if necessary, application of specific chemical substances according to medical indication.

The intensity is adapted according to the skin type and the treatment objective (superficial or medium). A slight burning or tight sensation may be felt, which is completely tolerable.

üõ° Post-care

  • Strict use of SPF 50+ sunscreen.
  • Avoid direct sun for at least one week.
  • Do not use exfoliants or irritating products for 3-5 days.
  • There may be slight peeling or redness in the first few days.
