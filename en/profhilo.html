Profhilo: deep hydration and real firmness, without fillers

When you start noticing that your skin no longer looks as luminous, or that it has lost firmness even if you don't have deep wrinkles, Profhilo may be the ideal treatment. It is a bioremodeling with ultrapure hyaluronic acid that acts from within, improving the quality, elasticity, and hydration of the skin without changing your features or adding volume.

Unlike fillers, Profhilo stimulates collagen and elastin, helping to recover that natural freshness you feel is being lost. It is perfect if you are looking for a gentle lifting effect, with results that are seen quickly.

What is it used for?

This treatment is performed to improve the appearance of aged and dehydrated skin, reduce laxity, and provide luminosity. Rapid and natural results

Unlike other biostimulators, Profhilo offers visible results in less time. In just two sessions, spaced one month apart, a significant improvement in skin firmness and elasticity is obtained. In addition, its high concentration of hyaluronic acid allows for deep hydration without providing unnecessary volume.

Profhilo not only hydrates the skin but also stimulates the production of type I, III, IV, and VII collagen, which progressively improves the dermal structure. Its diffusion capacity allows it to act homogeneously, providing a natural rejuvenating effect without leaving overloaded areas.

Differences with other treatments

Unlike Skinboosters, which require multiple applications in different areas to achieve deep hydration, Profhilo is applied in only five strategic points per side of the face, achieving homogeneous coverage. Its biostimulating action makes it an ideal option for those seeking to improve skin texture without resorting to conventional fillers.

How is the procedure?

During the session, small injections are applied in strategic points of the face or the area to be treated. The procedure lasts approximately 30 minutes and does not require anesthesia, although anesthetic cream can be applied if the patient wishes. After the treatment, daily activities can be resumed without problems, as it leaves no visible marks or requires recovery time.

Results and duration

The effects of Profhilo begin to be noticed from the first session, with more hydrated, luminous, and firm skin. However, optimal results are achieved after the second application, when the skin has completed the biostimulation process.

The duration of the treatment varies according to skin type, but generally, the effects are maintained between 6 and 12 months. To prolong the benefits, maintenance sessions are recommended every 6 months, according to specialist indication.
