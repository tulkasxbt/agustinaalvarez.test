What is Long Lasting?

Long Lasting is a latest generation biostimulator designed to hydrate and regenerate skin deeply. Unlike other hyaluronic acid treatments, Long Lasting not only hydrates, but also stimulates collagen production, achieving firmer, brighter, and more elastic skin with prolonged effects over time.

What is Long Lasting for?

  • Deep hydration: Acts from the inner layers of the skin to restore natural hydration.
  • Tensor effect: Stimulates collagen and elastin production, improving skin firmness.
  • Aging prevention: Regular use helps delay the appearance of wrinkles and sagging.
  • Tone and texture improvement: Unifies the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines and open pores.
  • Natural results: Does not alter facial structure, but enhances natural beauty.

How is Long Lasting applied?

The procedure is fast, safe, and almost painless. Microinjections are applied into the dermis with an ultrafine needle. The session lasts approximately **30 minutes** and requires no recovery time.

Risks and side effects

While Long Lasting is a safe treatment, it is normal that mild redness or small bruises may appear in the treated area, which disappear in **24 to 48 hours**. To minimize risks, we always perform a prior evaluation to ensure the treatment is suitable for you.

Results and maintenance

Results begin to be noticed from the **second week**, with visibly brighter and healthier skin. A protocol of **2 to 3 initial sessions** spaced by a month is recommended, followed by maintenance every **6 to 9 months**.
