What is a Skinbooster?

The skinbooster treatment is an advanced facial rejuvenation technique that deeply hydrates the skin from within. It consists of the application of hyaluronic acid microinjections, a natural component of skin that helps improve its elasticity, texture, and luminosity. Unlike facial fillers, skinboosters are not designed to add volume, but to revitalize and rehydrate the skin, giving it a fresher and more youthful look.

Why it is done

The skinbooster is done to treat dehydration and loss of skin elasticity. It is ideal for those who want to improve the quality of their skin without changing their facial features. This treatment is especially beneficial for people with dull or dry skin, fine lines, and those seeking a natural rejuvenating effect without the need for invasive procedures. A clear example is when you notice your face skin looks tired or lifeless, the skinbooster can give it a hydration "boost" so it looks radiant again.

Risks

As with any injection procedure, the skinbooster can cause some mild and temporary side effects, such as redness, swelling, or small bruises at injection points. These usually disappear in a few days. Although rare, there is a risk of allergic reaction to hyaluronic acid, so it is always important to perform the treatment with a trained professional who can handle any complications.

How you prepare

Before skinbooster treatment, it is recommended to avoid alcohol consumption and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) like aspirin, as they may increase the risk of bruising. Additionally, it is important to keep the skin clean and avoid using irritating products or aggressive treatments in the days prior to the procedure.

During the procedure

The procedure is relatively simple and quick. Hyaluronic acid microinjections are applied to the superficial layers of the facial skin. The number of injections and their depth will depend on your skin's needs and the area to be treated. The procedure lasts approximately 30 minutes and is generally well tolerated by patients, although topical anesthesia can be applied to minimize any discomfort.

Results

Skinbooster results begin to be noticeable after a few weeks, when the skin starts to look more hydrated, smooth, and luminous. The treatment can be done in maintenance sessions every 6 months to preserve the effects. In many cases, an initial cycle of 2 to 3 sessions spaced by one month is recommended to obtain optimal results. An example of the benefits is seeing how fine lines and dryness in the skin visibly improve, giving you a fresher and rejuvenated appearance.
