Overview

Forehead wrinkles are one of the most common signs of facial aging. They usually appear due to the loss of collagen and elastin in the skin, which causes expression lines to become more visible over time. These wrinkles can also be accentuated by factors such as sun exposure and stress. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer various effective treatments to smooth these wrinkles and regain a younger and more relaxed appearance.

Why do I have forehead wrinkles and how can they be eliminated?

  • Natural aging: Over time, the skin loses its ability to produce collagen and elastin, which causes wrinkle formation. Treatments like Botox and hyaluronic acid fillers are ideal for smoothing expression lines on the forehead.
  • Environmental factors: Sun exposure without adequate protection can accelerate skin aging. In these cases, we recommend combining treatments like chemical peels and platelet-rich plasma (PRP) to improve skin texture and luminosity.

What type of treatments exist to improve forehead wrinkles?

At our Aesthetic Center, we offer a wide range of treatments to reduce forehead wrinkles without surgery. Among the most popular are:

  • Botox: One of the most effective treatments to relax the muscles responsible for wrinkles.
  • Hyaluronic acid fillers: Fill deeper lines and improve lost volume in the forehead area.
  • Radiofrequency with Accent Prime: Stimulates collagen production, improving skin firmness.

Do forehead wrinkles worsen over time?

Yes, forehead wrinkles tend to get deeper over time if left untreated. Loss of skin elasticity and constant repetition of facial expressions can make these expression lines permanent. Treatments like Botox or radiofrequency with Accent Prime can prevent the advancement of wrinkles and improve their current appearance.

What type of forehead wrinkles are there?

  • Horizontal lines: These are the wrinkles that form across the forehead and are mainly caused by repetitive facial expressions.
  • Vertical expression lines: Also known as "frown lines", they appear between the eyebrows and tend to accentuate with age and stress.

What results can I expect with the treatments you offer?

The results of the treatments vary according to the chosen method and the individual characteristics of each patient. In general, you can expect a noticeable reduction in wrinkle depth and an overall improvement in skin appearance. Treatments like Botox and hyaluronic acid fillers provide immediate results, while Sculptra and Radiesse offer long-term improvements by stimulating collagen production. At our Aesthetic Center, we adapt each treatment to the individual needs of our patients to achieve optimal results.
