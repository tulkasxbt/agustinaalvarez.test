Overview

Gummy smile is a condition where an excessive amount of gum is shown when smiling, which can cause discomfort in those who suffer from it. This may be due to hyperactivity in the upper lip elevator muscle or excessive gum height. At our Aesthetic Center, we have advanced treatments to correct this condition, achieving a more harmonious and aesthetic smile.

Why do I have a gummy smile and how can it be eliminated?

  • Hyperactivity of the upper lip elevator muscle: The lip rises more than normal when smiling, exposing an excessive amount of gum. Treatments like botox can relax it, reducing gum exposure.
  • Imbalance in lip and gum length: In some cases, a short lip or large gums can generate this condition. Combined treatments with hyaluronic acid fillers can help improve aesthetics.

What type of treatments exist to improve gummy smile?

We offer a series of treatments at our Aesthetic Center to correct gummy smile. Some of the most effective are:

  • Botox: Helps relax the upper lip elevator muscle, reducing gum exposure when smiling.
  • Hyaluronic acid fillers: Used to give more volume and improve the proportion between the lip and gums.

Does gummy smile worsen over time?

Gummy smile usually does not worsen over time, but it can continue to be a source of discomfort if left untreated. With botox and hyaluronic acid fillers, a long-lasting and effective correction can be achieved.

What types of gummy smile are there?

  • Muscular hyperactivity: It is the most common cause, where the upper lip rises more than usual.
  • Gum excess: In some cases, the gums are more prominent than usual, giving an appearance of gummy smile.

What results can I expect with the treatments offered?

Results are usually visible in a short time. With botox, effects can last between 4 and 6 months, while hyaluronic acid fillers can offer a longer-lasting solution. The goal is to achieve a more balanced and natural smile.
