Overview

Facial aging is a natural process that affects everyone, marked by the appearance of wrinkles, loss of volume, and skin sagging. Over time, factors such as the reduction of collagen and elastin, sun exposure, and hormonal changes play an important role in these visible signs of aging. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer various treatments to combat the signs of aging, helping to rejuvenate and improve the appearance of the face.

Why do I have facial aging and how can it be eliminated?

  • Natural factors: Natural aging is inevitable, but there are treatments that can slow down and reverse its effects. Options such as Botox, hyaluronic acid fillers, and Sculptra are highly effective for maintaining a fresh and youthful face.
  • Sun damage: Sun exposure without adequate protection accelerates skin aging. We recommend chemical peels or Q-Switch laser to reduce spots and improve texture.
  • Lifestyle: Factors such as lack of sleep, stress, and a poor diet can contribute to premature aging. In these cases, treatments like PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) or facial mesotherapy help revitalize the skin.

What type of treatments exist to improve facial aging?

At our Aesthetic Center, we offer a variety of personalized treatments to combat facial aging without surgery. Some of the most effective treatments include:

  • Hyaluronic Acid Fillers: Restore lost volume and correct deep wrinkles and folds.
  • Radiofrequency with Accent Prime: This non-invasive treatment helps tighten the skin and improve its texture by stimulating collagen.
  • Sculptra: Stimulates collagen production, improving skin firmness and elasticity.
  • Botox: Ideal for reducing expression lines and dynamic wrinkles on the forehead, between the eyebrows, and crow's feet.

Does facial aging worsen over time?

Yes, facial aging tends to advance over time if not treated properly. Factors such as continued sun exposure and lack of skin care accelerate the process. However, with appropriate treatments such as Profhilo, Radiesse, and Accent Prime radiofrequency, it is possible to prevent and reverse the visible signs of aging.

What types of facial aging are there?

  • Dynamic wrinkles: Caused by repetitive facial movements, such as expression lines on the forehead. Treatments like Botox are ideal for smoothing them.
  • Static wrinkles: These wrinkles are visible even when the face is at rest. To treat them, hyaluronic acid fillers or Sculptra are recommended.
  • Volume loss: With age, the face can lose volume, especially in the cheeks and jawline. Treatments like Radiesse and Sculptra help restore facial contours.

What results can I expect with the treatments you offer?

The results of our facial aging treatments are visible gradually, depending on the chosen treatment. With Botox, effects are quick, while Sculptra and Radiesse offer progressive improvements in collagen production. generally, you can expect a notable improvement in the firmness, texture, and overall appearance of your skin. Additionally, treatment with Profhilo provides deep hydration and stimulates elastin and collagen production, improving skin quality in the long term. At our Aesthetic Center, we create personalized treatment plans for each patient, guaranteeing natural and long-lasting results.
