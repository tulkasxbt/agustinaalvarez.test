Overview

Cellulite is a very common condition that mainly affects women. It is characterized by the appearance of "dimples" or irregularities in the skin, especially in areas such as the thighs, buttocks, and abdomen. The main cause of cellulite is the accumulation of fat in these areas, along with a lack of skin firmness. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer advanced treatments to improve the appearance of cellulite and restore firmness to your skin.

Why do I have cellulite and how can it be eliminated?

  • Hormonal factors: Hormonal changes, especially during puberty, pregnancy, and menopause, can contribute to the appearance of cellulite. Treatments like radiofrequency with Accent Prime can help improve skin firmness.
  • Genetics: Genetic predisposition also influences the appearance of cellulite. While genetics cannot be changed, treatments like Accent Prime treatment can significantly reduce the appearance of cellulite.
  • Lifestyle: Lack of exercise, a poorly balanced diet, and fluid retention can aggravate cellulite. Incorporating specialized treatments can be key to improving skin appearance.

What type of treatments exist to improve cellulite?

At our Aesthetic Center, we have various treatments to address cellulite effectively:

  • Accent Prime Radiofrequency: This treatment combines ultrasound and radiofrequency technology to reduce localized fat and improve skin texture.
  • Mesotherapy: Consists of microinjections of substances that help dissolve fat and improve skin appearance.
  • Laser: We use advanced laser technologies to improve skin elasticity and reduce the appearance of cellulite.

Does cellulite get worse over time?

While cellulite is a chronic condition that does not disappear on its own, it can worsen over time if not treated properly. Factors such as aging, weight gain, and lack of treatment can aggravate the appearance of the skin. That is why at our aesthetic center we recommend starting with treatments such as Accent Prime radiofrequency as soon as possible.

What types of cellulite are there?

  • Soft cellulite: Characterized by skin that feels loose or soft, generally associated with loss of muscle tone.
  • Hard cellulite: This type of cellulite appears in firmer skin and is usually more difficult to treat.
  • Edematous cellulite: Associated with fluid retention and is usually the most visible and painful.

What results can I expect with the treatments you offer?

Results vary depending on the type of treatment and the severity of cellulite, but in general, patients experience a significant improvement in skin texture and firmness. With treatments like Accent Prime radiofrequency, results are usually visible after several sessions, with a noticeable reduction in cellulite and smoother, more toned skin.
