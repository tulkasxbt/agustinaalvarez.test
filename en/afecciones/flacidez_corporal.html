Overview

Body flaccidity is a common condition that affects skin firmness in various areas of the body, such as arms, abdomen, thighs, and buttocks. This loss of firmness can be related to aging, rapid weight loss, or simply genetic factors. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer a variety of treatments to combat flaccidity and improve body contours.

Why do I have body flaccidity and how can it be eliminated?

  • Loss of collagen and elastin: As we age, our skin loses its ability to stay firm due to the decrease of these essential proteins. Body radiofrequency treatments are ideal for stimulating collagen production and tightening the skin.
  • Rapid weight loss: After significant weight loss, skin may become lax. In these cases, we combine Accent Prime to improve flaccidity in areas like the abdomen and thighs.
  • Genetic factors: Some people are more prone to flaccidity due to their genetics. In our center, we design a personalized plan that includes treatments like mesotherapy to revitalize the skin.

What type of treatments exist to improve body flaccidity?

At our Aesthetic Center, we have non-invasive and effective treatments to combat body flaccidity. The most popular include:

  • Accent Prime: Advanced radiofrequency technology to tighten skin and improve body contours.
  • Mesotherapy: Revitalizing treatment that helps improve skin quality and firmness.
  • Body Radiofrequency: Stimulates collagen production, significantly improving skin texture.

Does flaccidity worsen over time?

If left untreated, body flaccidity can worsen due to continued loss of collagen and elasticity in the skin. Factors like sun exposure and aging accelerate this process. That's why it is fundamental to treat it in time with technologies like Accent Prime and body radiofrequency, which help prevent further skin deterioration.

What types of body flaccidity are there?

  • Skin flaccidity: Refers to skin laxity in areas like arms, thighs, and abdomen.
  • Muscle flaccidity: Occurs when muscles also lose firmness, something that can be addressed with muscle toning treatments combined with radiofrequency.

What results can I expect with the treatments you offer?

Results of treatments for body flaccidity vary according to the severity of the case and the treatment used. However, in general, you can expect a significant improvement in skin firmness and texture. Treatments like Accent Prime offer visible results after a few sessions, while others like body mesotherapy help revitalize the skin over several sessions.
