Overview

Crow's feet are expression lines that appear around the eyes, mainly caused by repetitive movements when smiling and natural aging. These lines tend to deepen over time due to the loss of collagen and elastin in the skin. At our Aesthetic Center, we have various treatments to smooth crow's feet and achieve a fresher and rejuvenated appearance.

Why do I have crow's feet and how can they be eliminated?

  • Natural aging: Collagen production decreases with age, leading to the appearance of these wrinkles. Treatments like Botox and hyaluronic acid fillers are ideal options to smooth these lines and return elasticity to the skin.
  • External factors: Prolonged sun exposure and lack of hydration can also aggravate crow's feet. In these cases, we recommend combining chemical peels and platelet rich plasma (PRP) to revitalize and nourish the skin.

What type of treatments exist to improve crow's feet?

At our Aesthetic Center, we offer various treatment options to improve the appearance of crow's feet. Some of the most effective include:

  • Botox: One of the most used treatments to relax muscles around the eyes and smooth wrinkles.
  • Hyaluronic Acid Fillers: Help restore lost volume and reduce line depth.
  • Accent Prime Facial Radiofrequency: Stimulates collagen production in deep layers, improving skin firmness.
  • Chemical Peel: Exfoliates superficial skin layers to reduce wrinkles and improve texture.

Do crow's feet worsen over time?

Yes, crow's feet tend to deepen over time if not treated. Continuous sun exposure, along with natural decrease of collagen and elastin, can worsen the situation. Treatments like Botox or hyaluronic acid fillers help prevent further deterioration and keep skin smoother and fresher.

What types of crow's feet exist?

  • Dynamic crow's feet: Are lines that appear when making facial expressions, like smiling.
  • Static crow's feet: Are wrinkles that remain visible even when the face is at rest.

What results can I expect with the treatments offered?

Results of crow's feet treatments may vary according to the chosen method. Treatments like Botox offer visible results in a few days, while hyaluronic acid fillers provide an immediate effect on volume and smoothing of lines. At our Aesthetic Center, we evaluate each case personally to achieve the best possible result for each patient. Additionally, treatment with Sculptra stimulates collagen production, providing a progressive and long-lasting effect in improving skin texture and firmness.
