Treatment for spots and melasma with Q-Switch Laser

If you have dark spots, melasma or uneven skin tone and have already tried creams without lasting results, this treatment may be for you. We use Q-Switch technology with Harmony Pro equipment, a safe and effective laser that acts selectively on the pigment without damaging the surrounding skin.

The goal is not only to lighten, but to achieve brighter, more even and firmer skin, activating your collagen naturally. It is an ideal alternative if you are looking for progressive rejuvenation without invasive interventions.

How many sessions do you need?

The number of sessions varies according to the condition to be treated, but generally between 3 and 6 sessions are recommended with an interval of 4 weeks between each one. Results are progressive and reach their maximum effect approximately 3 months after treatment.

Results and Benefits

After completing the treatment, a firmer, brighter and more uniform skin is observed. In addition to improving texture, the Q-Switch Laser helps eliminate persistent spots and soften skin tone irregularities.
