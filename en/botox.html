Botox: your skin smoother, without losing naturalness

If you feel like expression lines are starting to mark you more than you'd like, Botox can help you prevent and smooth out those wrinkles without changing your essence. It is a safe medical procedure, performed with botulinum toxin type A, which acts by temporarily relaxing the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles, especially on the forehead, frown lines, and crow's feet.

The result: skin that is more rested, luminous, and fresh, without losing your expression or naturalness. In our center, each application is personalized and performed by medical professionals, so you feel safe and supported at all times.

What is Botox for?

  • Dynamic wrinkles: Smooths expression lines on the forehead, frown lines, and eye contour.
  • Bruxism: Relaxes jaw muscles, reducing pain and dental wear.
  • Hyperhidrosis: Decreases excessive sweating in armpits, hands, and feet.
  • Lifting effect: Allows lifting eyebrows and improving facial harmony without surgery.
  • Gummy smile: Corrects excessive gum exposure when smiling.

Botox vs. Hyaluronic Acid Filler

It is common to confuse these two treatments, but each has a specific function:

  • Botox: Relaxes muscles to reduce dynamic wrinkles (forehead, frown lines, crow's feet).
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Fills and volumizes areas like lips, nasolabial folds, and cheekbones.

In many cases, these treatments are combined to achieve comprehensive facial rejuvenation, adapted to the needs of each patient.

How long do the effects of Botox last?

The effects of Botox begin to be noticed between 2 and 7 days after application, with optimal results in 2 weeks. The duration of the effect is 3 to 5 months, depending on individual factors such as metabolism, the treated area, and application frequency.

Is Botox treatment safe?

Yes. When applied by a certified medical professional, Botox is a safe and predictable procedure.

However, it is important to keep in mind:

  • Possible side effects: Mild bruising, swelling, or sensitivity in the application area.
  • Do not massage the treated area: To avoid product migration.
  • Reversible results: If the patient is not satisfied, the effects will disappear over time.
