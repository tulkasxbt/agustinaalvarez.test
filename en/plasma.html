What is Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP)?

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) is an innovative treatment that harnesses the regenerative properties of your own blood to improve skin quality and stimulate collagen production. Through a process of extraction and centrifugation, a platelet concentrate is obtained and injected into specific areas of the face or scalp, promoting cell regeneration and revitalizing the skin naturally.

What is it performed for?

PRP is ideal for those seeking a natural and safe treatment for facial and hair rejuvenation. It is used to:

  • Improve skin texture and firmness: Reduces expression lines, fine wrinkles, and provides luminosity.
  • Scar treatment: Helps attenuate acne marks and skin scars.
  • Hair regeneration: Revitalizes hair follicles and strengthens hair growth.

How to prepare for the treatment

To ensure the best quality of plasma and minimize side effects, it is recommended:

  • Dairy fast 3 hours before the treatment.
  • Stay well hydrated to optimize blood extraction.
  • Do not use irritating or exfoliating products on the skin the previous days.

During the procedure

The PRP treatment begins with the extraction of a small amount of blood, similar to a lab test. Then, the blood is processed in a centrifuge to separate the platelet-rich plasma. Subsequently, this concentrate is injected into the areas to be treated with a fine needle, either on the face or scalp. The procedure is quick, lasting between 45 minutes and an hour, and requires no recovery time.

PRP Results

The effects of PRP are progressive. In facial treatment, initial results can be noticed starting at three weeks, observing a more luminous, firmer, and rejuvenated skin. In the case of hair PRP, the improvement in hair density and thickness usually becomes visible in a period of 3 to 6 months, with a progressive strengthening of the follicles.

To optimize and prolong results, a plan of periodic sessions is recommended, with maintenance every 6 to 12 months according to each patient's need.
