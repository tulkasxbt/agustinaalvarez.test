What is Facial Mesotherapy?

Facial mesotherapy is an aesthetic treatment that uses microinjections of a cocktail of vitamins, minerals, amino acids, and hyaluronic acid to improve the skin from within. Its goal is to hydrate, brighten, and firm the face, providing a rejuvenated and healthy appearance.

French facial mesotherapy follows the same principle, but with specific high-quality products used in Europe. It is recognized for its natural and sophisticated results, achieving subtle rejuvenation without altering features.
