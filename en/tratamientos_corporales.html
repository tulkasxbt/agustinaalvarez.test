Body Treatments

Body Modeling

Body modeling is a non-invasive treatment that helps reduce localized adiposity, improve skin appearance, and achieve a more sculpted figure. Using advanced technology like Accent Prime, this procedure can help lower abdominal fat and define problem areas.

Cellulite Treatment

Cellulite treatment is designed to reduce cellulite dimples and improve the appearance of uneven skin. With technology like Accent Prime, cellulite and localized adiposity can be targeted, achieving firmer and more uniform skin.

Laxity Treatment

Laxity treatment is ideal for those seeking to regain their skin's firmness after weight loss or simply due to the passage of time. Using technology like Accent Prime, laxity can be improved, reducing the appearance of loose skin and improving overall texture.
