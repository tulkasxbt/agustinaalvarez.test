What is Permanent Hair Removal?

Unwanted hair on the face is a common concern in both men and women, and can be related to various factors, such as hormonal changes, genetics, or the use of certain medications. This condition can affect self-esteem and the overall appearance of the face. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer an effective and lasting solution for this problem: permanent hair removal with the Soprano Ice equipment, an innovative and safe method to eliminate unwanted hair progressively.

Why do I have unwanted facial hair and how can it be eliminated?

  • Hormonal factors: Changes in hormonal balance, such as in menopause or polycystic ovary syndrome, can cause excessive facial hair growth. Permanent hair removal with Soprano Ice offers a lasting and effective solution for these cases.
  • Genetics: Some people have a genetic predisposition to greater facial hair growth. Treatment with Soprano Ice can reduce hair significantly, providing lasting results.
  • External factors: The use of certain medications or hormonal treatments can also trigger unwanted hair growth on the face. Hair removal with Soprano Ice is ideal for reducing these unwanted effects.

What type of treatments exist to eliminate unwanted facial hair?

At our Aesthetic Center, the most effective treatment to eliminate unwanted facial hair is permanent hair removal with Soprano Ice. This state-of-the-art equipment uses laser technology that reduces hair by 40% from the first session, is safe, fast and suitable for all skin types, including the most sensitive. With each session, hair is significantly reduced, achieving smoother skin free of unwanted hair.

Does unwanted facial hair worsen over time?

If left untreated, unwanted facial hair can become thicker and darker over time, especially if related to hormonal factors. Soprano Ice hair removal not only eliminates existing hair, but also prevents future growth, offering a lasting solution.

What types of unwanted hair are there?

  • Fine hair: Light and soft hair is more difficult to eliminate, but Soprano Ice is effective in its progressive reduction.
  • Thick hair: Dark and thick hair is more visible and is effectively eliminated with Soprano Ice, offering fast and lasting results.

What results can I expect with the treatment we offer?

With the permanent hair removal treatment with Soprano Ice, results are observed from the first session, achieving up to 40% hair reduction. Most patients experience smoother skin after a few sessions. The treatment is painless and designed for sensitive skin. Frequently Asked Questions

Is permanent hair removal treatment safe?
Yes, permanent hair removal treatment is safe when performed with approved technology and by trained professionals. We follow strict protocols to care for your skin in each session.
Can it damage the skin or leave scars?
No, modern equipment is designed to act on the follicle without damaging the surrounding skin. Although there may be mild redness, it disappears in a few hours and leaves no marks.
Does the treatment hurt?
The level of pain is tolerable and depends on each person. Some areas may be more sensitive, but most describe the sensation as a mild heat or momentary prick.
How many sessions are necessary?
Between 6 and 8 sessions are needed, although this may vary depending on each person and area to be treated. Sessions are scheduled with specific intervals to obtain better results.
Is it worth it in the long run?
Yes, permanent hair removal is an investment that allows you to forget about other methods such as wax or razors. In the long run, you save time and money, in addition to achieving hair-free skin.
Does it work for all skin types and hair colors?
Yes, although some lighter hair colors may require specific technologies. In general, the treatment is effective for most skin and hair tones.
What care should I take before and after the treatment?
Avoid sun exposure and the use of irritating products in the treated area. After the session, it is recommended to apply sunscreen and avoid intense heat.
What are the common side effects?
They may include mild redness or swelling, which disappear in a few hours. Our team is prepared to minimize any discomfort.
Does permanent hair removal eliminate hair 100%?
Permanent hair removal reduces hair growth significantly. Some may experience very fine hair or small areas where hair may appear in the future.
Can it be done on any part of the body?
Yes, permanent hair removal is suitable for almost all areas of the body, including the most sensitive areas such as the face or bikini area.
