Overview

Wrinkles are creases that appear on the skin over time, mainly affecting the face, neck, and other exposed areas. These form due to the decrease of collagen and elastin, which are responsible for the firmness and elasticity of the skin. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer several treatments to soften and reduce wrinkles, restoring a more youthful appearance to your skin.

Why do I have wrinkles and how can they be eliminated?

  • Natural aging: As we facilitate, collagen production decreases, which generates wrinkles. Treatments like Botox and hyaluronic acid fillers are very effective in softening them.
  • Environmental factors: Sun exposure and pollution also accelerate the aging process. To counteract these effects, we recommend procedures such as chemical peeling and platelet-rich plasma (PRP).

What type of treatments exist to improve wrinkles?

At our Aesthetic Center, we have a variety of treatments to improve wrinkles, without the need for surgery. Some of the most prominent are:

  • Hyaluronic acid fillers: Restore lost volume, filling deep wrinkles.
  • Sculptra: Stimulates collagen production to improve texture and firmness.
  • Radiofrequency with Accent Prime: Firms the skin and improves general appearance.
  • Radiesse: A biostimulator that restores volume and stimulates collagen production.
  • Botox: Ideal for softening expression lines and dynamic wrinkles.

Do wrinkles worsen over time?

Yes, wrinkles tend to worsen over time if not treated. Factors such as sun exposure and natural aging accelerate this process. However, with treatments like Botox or platelet-rich plasma (PRP), their deepening can be prevented.

What type of wrinkles are there?

  • Dynamic wrinkles: Produced by repeated contraction of facial muscles, such as expression lines.
  • Static wrinkles: Develop over time due to loss of collagen and elasticity.

What results can I expect with the treatments you offer?

Results of wrinkle treatments vary depending on the type of wrinkle and the treatment used. In general, treatments like Botox offer almost immediate visible results, while others like Sculptra and Radiesse stimulate collagen production, with progressive long-term results. At our Aesthetic Center, we focus on designing a personalized plan for each patient.
