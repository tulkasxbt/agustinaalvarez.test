Overview

The double chin, is an accumulation of fat or skin below the chin that can appear due to various factors such as genetics, weight gain or loss of elasticity due to aging. At our Aesthetic Center, we offer a variety of effective and non-invasive treatments to reduce double chin and improve facial contour, providing natural and harmonious results.

Why do I have a double chin and how can it be eliminated?

  • Genetic factors: Some people develop a double chin due to genetics, regardless of body weight. Treatments like PB Serum enzymes or Accent Prime radiofrequency can help reduce localized fat in this area and improve firmness.
  • Weight gain: Excess fat in the double chin may be related to weight gain. Mesotherapy is ideal for dissolving fat deposits and defining the chin contour.
  • Aging: Over time, the skin loses elasticity and causes sagging in the chin area. Treatments like Sculptra or Radiesse are excellent for stimulating collagen production and improving skin firmness.

What type of treatments exist to improve double chin?

At our Aesthetic Center, we have a wide range of non-invasive treatments to treat double chin, providing natural and progressive results. Some of the most recommended treatments are:

  • PB Serum Enzymes: Ideal for dissolving localized fat effectively and without surgery.
  • Accent Prime Facial Radiofrequency: Stimulates collagen production, reducing flaccidity and improving facial contour.
  • Sculptra: Restores firmness by stimulating long-term collagen production.
  • Mesotherapy: Helps dissolve fat deposits in the double chin, achieving a more defined chin.

Does double chin worsen over time?

Without treatment, double chin can worsen over time due to fat accumulation or loss of elasticity from aging. It is important to treat it on time to prevent it from getting worse. Treatments like Accent Prime radiofrequency or PB Serum enzymes help control and reduce double chin safely.

What types of double chin are there?

  • Fatty double chin: Produced by subcutaneous fat accumulation. Treated effectively with mesotherapy or PB Serum enzymes.
  • Flaccid double chin: Related to loss of elasticity and firmness in the skin. Accent Prime facial radiofrequency and Sculptra treatments are ideal for improving skin structure.

What results can I expect with the treatments offered?

Results vary depending on the type of treatment used and the individual characteristics of each patient. However, in general, a visible reduction in double chin volume and an improvement in skin firmness can be expected. Treatments like PB Serum enzymes and mesotherapy provide progressive effects, while Accent Prime radiofrequency helps tighten the skin. At our Aesthetic Center, we create a personalized plan for each patient, ensuring results that adapt to their needs.
